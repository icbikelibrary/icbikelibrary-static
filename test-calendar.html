Are you currently accepting donations?
Yes, and Thank you! You can drop donations at 1222 South Gilbert Court ANYTIME DAY OR NIGHT--just use the locks provided on the bike rack out front to lock them up. We are open Wednesdays and Fridays from 12 to 5 pm and Saturdays 10 - 3 if you want to drop them off when someone is here, and we can also give you a tax deductible receipt at this time. 
Are you checking out bikes? 
We are checking out bikes on a first come, first serve basis. We are open for Bicycle Checkout WEDNESDAY & FRIDAY 12 - 5 PM AND SATURDAYS 10 - 3 PM. 

Step 1: Determine if you want a Checkout or an As-is bike. A Checkout Bike is a 6-month rental with the option to buy. For $100 - $300 you get a bike for 6 months. YOU CHOOSE if you want to return the bike for a refund minus $50 OR KEEP THE BIKE and we keep your deposit. We sell As-is bikes too. Those are final sale with no trade-ins or returns. Our higher-end As-is bikes (over $300) are listed on the website; most of the lower-end bikes (~$20-$300) are not listed and you can come in the shop and browse them when we are open. All of the lower-end As-is bikes will need to be fixed in some capacity. 

Step 2: Look at our inventory of Checkout Bikes. If there are none listed then we are currently out of Checkout bikes. Check back again later. Look at our inventory of higher-end As-is bikes if that is an option that interests you.
​
Step 3: Come by 1222 South Gilbert Court Wednesday or Friday from 12 - 5 or Saturdays 10 - 3. 
CAN I BUY AN AS-IS BIKE?
We do have High-end As-Is bikes for sale. We post a few of them here, and regularly on Instagram and Facebook. Most of the lower-end As-is bikes are not listed on the website. You can come look at the As-is bikes in the shop when we are open. 
What is a Checkout Bike?
  • Bike has been refurbished by a staff or volunteer mechanic
  • Bike has been final checked and test ridden by a second staff or volunteer mechanic
  • Bike is READY to ride
  • Bike qualifies for the checkout program (i.e., you have the option to return it within six months for a return on your deposit minus the sustainability fee).
Go to our Checkout Bikes page for more information!
