​About

​The Bike Library started in 2004 as a volunteer-run community bike project in Iowa City, Iowa with a mission of getting more people on bikes. We repair donated bikes and offer them to the public for check-out or purchase. Our socially conscious programs, beginner-friendly community rides and events make bicycling accessible to everyone in our community.
