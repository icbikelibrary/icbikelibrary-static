Rent-A Bench: Fix your bike!

Wednesday 12-5 PM, Friday 12-5 PM, Saturday 10-3 PM
FREE, $5 suggested donation

We are not a commercial bike shop and do not do repairs for-hire. Instead, we offer Rent-a-Bench!

Want to work on your own bike, but need tools, space, and some advice on what to do? Come to Rent-A-Bench! Our mechanicals offer guidance and one-on-one step-by-step instructions through bike service and repair. Here are some tips:
  • Give yourself plenty of time. Maintenance and repairs usually take longer than you think!
  • Instruction is offered on a first come, first basis. If your bike has an urgent issue, come as early as possible. 
  • Come in before your bike breaks down. If you hear something that doesn't sound right, bring it in! We are happy to help you diagnose issues. 
  • If you've identified the problem, Park Tool videos will familiarize you with the tools and process to expedite your project.

Even if you've never lubed your chain, pumped up the tires, or adjusted your brakes, these are things you can do! Do you just want to practice fixing a flat? Stop in during Rent-a-Bench and ask someone to walk through the steps with ya!
