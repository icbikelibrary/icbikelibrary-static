Time to Crank

In tandem with our end-of-year giving campaign, we'll be launching
The Pedal Cranker Riding Pledge CHALLENGE!
As we put this trying year behind us and round the corner into the coming one,
it's essential that everything we do remains instilled with the passion
that first brought the Bike Library to life,
the mission: to get more butts on bikes!

In keeping with our mission,
we'll be starting with our own butts!

We're a team of dedicated volunteer riders pledging to ride
200 miles for every $1,000 raised

How many miles have the Pedal Crankers cranked so far?


Keep up with the Crankers on social media under the hashtag
#BLpedalcrankers
Our incredible team of volunteers is always cranking to fix bikes, recycle bikes, checkout bikes, promote biking advocacy, empower new riders, and (let's not forget)
actually get out & ride!

Every mile ridden & every dollar raised
will help in our mission
to get affordable, high-quality
BIKES
to those who need them most.
